HOW TO PLAY ?
GAME RULES
  • You begin the quiz with 3 lives
  • You will be asked 10 Doom related questions
  • You will have 4 answers to choose from
  • Every wrong answer you give you lose a life
  • Answer 3 Questions right in a row to gain a life
  • Win by getting through the quiz without losing all 3 lives
  • The dashboard will show how many lives you have and you current streak
  • GOOD LUCK!!! You'll need it....
